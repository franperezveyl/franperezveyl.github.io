Francisca Pérez

Assistant Professor at the Business School of Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez.

I am a macroeconomist working primarily on labor economics. My research studies how households, workers, and firms adjust their behavior in response to constraints related to technology, labor market regulation, care responsibilities, and social norms. I am interested in understanding how these forces shape time allocation, occupational choices, and labor demand. I use rich microdata and a combination of causal inference and structural methods.
